ORGAN India has gone green!

In order to reduce our carbon footprint, and to conserve and protect natural resources for future generations, we are issuing only e-donor cards. You will receive your e-donor card with a unique Government registration number, an e-certificate, and an informational pamphlet, on the provided email address within 4 weeks. Please check your inbox/spam folder for our email. You may take a printout of the same if you wish.
