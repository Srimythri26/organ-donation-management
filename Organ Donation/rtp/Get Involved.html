PARTNER WITH US

India is silently suffering a public health crisis. Our country has among the lowest organ donation rates in the world.Data by National Organ and Tissue Transplantation Organisations suggests that an average of 500,000 people die every year,waiting for a life saving organ.Thus, public and private partnership can create a significant impact on India's organ donation landscape.

HOW YOU CAN HELP US

  • By conveying to other people the importance of organ donation and how they can help us sitting at home too by being socially active in various social media platforms and share organ donation information.

  • You can encourage people to register for being an organ donor by hosting a donor registration drive or adding links to the organ donor registry in social media platforms from time to time.

  • By providing us venues where we can hold several events so that more and more people get to know the importance of organ donation.
